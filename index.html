Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our companion platform? Explore how Thoryn fits into your life.

How does Thoryn "remember" users?

Thoryn uses a secure, managed database system stored on your personal node. This creates a persistent user memory. When you share details about your schedule, preferences, projects, family guidelines, or notes, Thoryn stores them. The next time you ask a question or request a task, Thoryn uses this memory to reply with high relevance, meaning you never have to repeat your preferences.

How does messaging via WhatsApp and Telegram work?

Thoryn operates dedicated, managed instances for WhatsApp and Telegram. Once you register for a Thoryn AI account, you can start chatting immediately. You have the flexibility to interact with your assistant through these messaging channels, our powerful web application, or our dedicated native apps — all synchronized with your personal node.

Can Thoryn compile files like presentations and PDFs autonomously?

Yes. Thoryn features built-in file compilers. You can ask the assistant to make a workout schedule, design study sheets, or structure a trip itinerary, and it will output printable PDF files, PowerPoint slide presentations, or interactive diagrams directly inside your chats.

What does "secured managed nodes" mean for my privacy?

With standard chatbots, your prompts, documents, and private chats are stored on corporate servers and often analyzed to train public AI models. Thoryn provisions a dedicated, isolated server node just for you. Your chats, memories, files, and links are fully private and never leaked or trained on.

Can I connect my existing calendar and email accounts?

Yes. Thoryn is designed to integrate seamlessly with standard calendar and email providers. Once connected, your assistant can autonomously check your availability, schedule meetings, summarize unread emails, and provide context-aware reminders based on your actual data.

Does Thoryn support multiple languages?

Absolutely. Thoryn is powered by high-performance multilingual models. You can chat, request research, and generate documents in over 40 languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, and Japanese, with full cultural and linguistic nuance.

Is there a mobile app I need to download?

You can, but you don't need to. While the primary way to interact with Thoryn is through WhatsApp or Telegram, our dedicated apps and web dashboard provide deep access to your Add-on dashboards, detailed analytics, and advanced node settings for more granular control.

How long does it take to set up my personal node?

Setup is nearly instantaneous. Once you secure a slot, our automated provisioning system deploys your dedicated node in less than 60 seconds. You'll receive your access credentials and simple instructions to link your messaging apps immediately.
